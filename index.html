Roadmap

As we continue to build and grow the Polygon Monkeys project, we're focusing on expanding our technological capabilities to provide even more value to the NFT ecosystem. Our roadmap includes plans to develop cutting-edge software tools for NFT creators, which will enable them to build their NFT projects after launch on the Polygon blockchain more easily and efficiently. We're also working on developing a scalable and decentralized infrastructure to support the rapidly growing NFT market. Our goal is to make the process of swapping and trading NFTs seamless, secure, and reliable. (P.S : We are not a launchpad/marketplace nor do we have the ambitions to become one.)
Additionally, we're exploring the integration of emerging technologies such as AI and machine learning into our platform to improve the user experience and create new, innovative applications for NFTs. We believe that the power of blockchain technology combined with these emerging technologies will lead to unprecedented levels of creativity and innovation in the NFT space.
Overall, we're committed to driving the development of the NFT ecosystem forward and providing creators and collectors with the most refined and user-friendly tools and platforms available. We're excited about the future of NFTs and the role that Polygon Monkeys will play in shaping it.
